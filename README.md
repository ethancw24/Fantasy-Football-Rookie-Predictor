# Fantasy-Football-Rookie-Predictor
Will use current top NFL player fantasy stats and their college stats to compare to current college football players to see if it can accurately predict the next fantasy stars.

Structure:
data/raw/                data/filtered/             model output
    │                         │                          │
Stage 1              Stage 2                Stage 3             Stage 4
Data Intake    →    Data Cleaning     →    Training &     →    Visualization
(APIs → CSVs)      (clean + merge)        Evaluation


Stuff to maybe take into account when looking at rookies:
Previous season record. Previous season Passer Rating. Previous season Rushing Rating. Prevoius Season Defenseive Rating. Previous season Offensive line rating. Look at season stats vs season averages (because of injury). Take note of head coaches.
